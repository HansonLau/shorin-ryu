Description

Fukyugata Ichi, the 1st kata. Sensei Shoshin Nagamine is the creator of this kata. The name Fukyugata means "to popularize". The first kata was made because the Pinan katas were too difficult for new students to learn. By creating Fukyugata Ichi, it kept more students interested since the difficulty was not as high. This kata introduces the moves Gedan Uke(low block), Jodan Uke(high block), Chudan Tsuki(middle punch), Seiken Tsuki(back/reverse punch), and Jodan Tsuki(high punch). This kata introduces the stances Zenkutsu Dachi(forward stance) and Shizentai Dachi(natural stance).
